# Wireframe-Level Page Sections

## Mobile-First UX Rules (All Pages)
- Sticky top bar includes phone icon + tap-to-call.
- Sticky bottom CTA bar on mobile: `Call`, `Book`, `Quote`.
- Keep critical CTAs visible above the fold.
- Use short section blocks and clear headings.
- Keep forms short with progressive disclosure.

## 1) Home Page
1. Emergency Banner (optional)
- Short strip at top:
  - `Emergency plumbing available. Call now.`
- CTA: `Call Now`

2. Header + Primary Navigation
- Logo
- Hamburger menu
- Prominent call button

3. Hero Section
- Headline focused on speed + trust + local service
- Subhead with service area mention
- Primary CTAs:
  - `Call Now`
  - `Book Online`
  - Secondary: `Get a Quote`

4. Trust Bar
- Badges/snippets:
  - Licensed & insured
  - Background-checked techs (if true)
  - Upfront pricing
  - Satisfaction guarantee

5. Core Services Snapshot
- 6 service cards with icon, short blurb, link to detail page
- CTA below grid: `View All Services`

6. How It Works (Conversion Flow)
- Step 1: Call or submit form
- Step 2: Confirm time and details
- Step 3: Technician arrives and resolves issue

7. Reviews Preview
- Rating average + count
- 3 testimonial cards
- CTA: `Read More Reviews`

8. Service Area Teaser
- Cities/neighborhoods list preview
- CTA: `Check Service Area`

9. Final CTA Band
- Headline: `Need a plumber today?`
- Buttons:
  - `Book Online`
  - `Call Now`

10. Footer
- Contact info, hours, quick links, legal links
